About GGSIPU – East Delhi Campus

The East Delhi Campus of Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University is a modern, multi-disciplinary campus focusing on emerging technologies, design, professional education and student-driven activities. The campus works closely with the Directorate of Students’ Welfare (DSW) to provide students with platforms for innovation, culture, technical societies and leadership.

EDC hosts flagship events like Elysian, Student Induction Programme (SIP), inter-school competitions, orientation programmes and outreach activities for the students of USAR, USCT, USDI, USAP and other constituent units.
