Directorate of Students’ Welfare – East Delhi Campus

Towards Holistic Growth of Every Student

The Directorate of Students Welfare was established to provide opportunities and adequate amenities for the all-round development of personality and leadership qualities among students of Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University.

Through scholarships, cultural and sports activities, student clubs and welfare initiatives, DSW nurtures responsible, confident and socially sensitive graduates.

Activities of DSW

  • Financial assistance to students belonging to the Economically Weaker Section (EWS).
  • Merit-cum-Means linked financial assistance to meritorious and needy students of Govt. of NCT of Delhi.
  • Travel Grant for participation in academic / cultural / sports events in India and abroad – covering travel, visa, registration and per diem as per University rules.
  • Seminar Grants to affiliated institutes of the University.
  • Educational / Excursion Tours for students of University Schools and Centres within India.
  • Financial assistance for Freshers’ and Farewell functions of University Schools.
  • Annual Cultural Fest “Anugoonj” (February) and Annual Inter-Collegiate Sports Meet (October).
  • Participation of University teams in tournaments organised by the Association of Indian Universities (AIU).
  • Cultural activities in association with SPIC MACAY and other cultural organisations.
  • Formation and monitoring of student clubs under Srijan – Indraprastha Society for Creative Expressions.

Clubs & Facilities

Under the banner of Srijan, multiple cultural and co-curricular clubs are constituted to provide platforms for music, dance, literature, fine arts, theatre, photography, social outreach and more.

Sports Facilities:

  • Dedicated courts and fields for Volleyball, Football, Cricket, Badminton, Basketball, Tennis, Kabaddi and Track & Field.
  • Open ground and canopy for cultural activities by clubs under Srijan.
  • Coaching & training under qualified and experienced coaches for major sports such as Football, Volleyball, Basketball, Tennis, Badminton, Kabaddi, Tug-of-War, Cricket, Track & Field, Yoga, Music and Dance.

Sports – East Delhi Campus

Sports and physical fitness are integral to student life at GGSIPU. The Sports Club of East Delhi Campus co-ordinates events, practice sessions and participation in University and AIU tournaments.

Annual Inter-Collegiate Sports Meet is organised in the month of October; major athletic and team events are held at University level.

Sports Activities

Inter-Collegiate tournaments are organised for various sports, with active participation from all affiliated institutes and University Schools. University teams also participate in National, State and University level tournaments.

Major Events

  • Volleyball (Men & Women)
  • Football (Men & Women)
  • Discus Throw & Shot Put (Men & Women)
  • Track events – 100m, 200m, 400m, 500m (Men & Women)
  • Cross Country Race & Long Jump (Men & Women)
  • Tug of War (Men & Women)
  • Cricket, Basketball, Badminton, Kabaddi
  • Body Building, Weightlifting / Power Lifting, Tennis

Coaching & Training Facilities

Special coaching and training is provided under the guidance of qualified and experienced coaches for the following disciplines:

  • Football, Volleyball, Basketball, Tennis, Badminton, Kabaddi, Tug-of-War
  • Cricket, Track & Field, Yoga
  • Music and Dance (for cultural competitions)
